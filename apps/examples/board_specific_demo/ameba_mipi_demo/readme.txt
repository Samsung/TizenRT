examples/mipi
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

  mipi and lcdc APIs are used for this demo
  This is the MIPI example AmebaSmart.

  This shows how to configure st7001s lcd screen with mipi and lcdc driver apis. In this example, the lcd will have 5 images switching, the maximum fps will be printed on top right corner

  Configs (see the details on Kconfig):
  * CONFIG_EXAMPLES_MIPI
